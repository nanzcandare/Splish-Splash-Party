# Google Drive Photo Setup Guide

## How to Get Direct Image Links from Google Drive

When you share a Google Drive folder, you'll need to convert the sharing links to direct image links that can be displayed on the website.

### Step 1: Share Your Google Drive Folder

1. Open your Google Drive folder with the photos
2. Right-click on the folder â†’ "Share" â†’ "Get link"
3. Set permissions to "Anyone with the link can view"
4. Copy the link and share it

### Step 2: Get Individual Photo Links

For EACH photo in the folder:
1. Right-click on the photo â†’ "Get link"
2. You'll get a link like: `https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ABC123xyz/view?usp=sharing`
3. Extract the FILE_ID (the part between `/d/` and `/view`)
4. Convert to direct link format: `https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=FILE_ID`

**Example:**
- Original: `https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ABC123xyz/view?usp=sharing`
- File ID: `1ABC123xyz`
- Direct link: `https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=1ABC123xyz`
